# CONTENT STRATEGY: KNOWLEDGE PRIMING UNDER COGNITIVE PRESSURE

**The Paradigm:**
*2 Second Witness* is NOT a trivia game. It is a psychometric instrument that uses real-world knowledge as the payload for cognitive stress tests. 

We do not ask the player to calmly select "Tutankhamun." We force the player to parse complex historical or medical data under severe temporal and perceptual constraints (e.g., Stroop interference, Signal vs. Noise, Rapid Classification). 

The goal is to test how fast the brain can retrieve and categorize factual knowledge when the sensory environment is actively hostile.

---

## 1. The Ontology Hierarchy
The endless scaling of content via GitHub is structured through semantic knowledge trees:
- **Universe:** The Broad Domain (e.g., `history`, `careers`, `current_events`, `trending`).
- **World:** The Specific Subject (e.g., `ancient_egypt`, `roman_empire`, `nursing`).
- **Scenario:** The Cognitive Spike applied to the Subject data.

## 2. Applying Knowledge to Cognitive Spikes
The 12 Flagship Cognitive Spikes remain unchanged mechanically, but their `prompt` and `target` data is drawn from the Knowledge Ontology.

### Example 1: Rapid Classification (Binary Sorting)
* **Universe:** Careers
* **World:** Nursing
* **The Spike:** The screen flashes a medical condition or treatment for 500ms (e.g., "Tachycardia"). 
* **The Constraint:** The player must rapidly sort it into `[Symptom]` or `[Treatment]` before the 2-second timer expires.

### Example 2: Stroop Test (Cognitive Interference)
* **Universe:** History
* **World:** Roman Empire
* **The Spike:** The word "PLEBEIAN" is displayed, but it is rendered in the color `GOLD`. 
* **The Constraint:** The player is instructed to click the button representing the *Color*, but the buttons are labeled with historical class structures ("PATRICIAN", "PLEBEIAN", "SENATOR"). The brain wants to read the text; the player must force it to see the color.

### Example 3: Signal vs Noise (Visual Search)
* **Universe:** History
* **World:** Ancient Egypt
* **The Spike:** 15 glowing, distracting hieroglyphs are scattered randomly across the void. 
* **The Constraint:** The player must locate the single "Eye of Horus" hidden among the noise and tap `[PRESENT]` or `[ABSENT]`.

## 3. The Continuous Rotation Strategy
- **Universe 1 (The Anchor):** The player's statistically "Most Played" or "Highest Affinity" Universe.
- **Universe 2 & 3 (The Discovery):** Pulled from the Top 100 Knowledge Categories dynamically updated via the GitHub LiveOps pipeline.
- **Universe 4, 5, 6 (The Edge):** Trending topics, ultra-niche careers, or experimental datasets pushed monthly to keep the cognitive challenges fresh.

This structure allows us to push infinite new factual data through the exact same 12 hard-coded psychological rendering manifolds.
