# IRIS ENGINE: THE PERCEPTUAL LENS API

**The Paradigm Shift:**
The Iris is not a portal. It is the optical subsystem of the platform. It renders classified data feeds under controlled cognitive stress.

## The Tri-Layer Architecture
1. **WITNESS ENGINE:** Logic (State, Progression, Truth Validation)
2. **IRIS ENGINE:** Perception (Lens, Intensity, Morphology)
3. **SCENARIO ENGINE:** Content (The 2-Second Data Feed)

## The Universal JSON Contract (Iris Lens Profile)

## The Engine Invariant
The following elements **never** change per Universe:
- The 2-Second Exposure Rule.
- The `P99` measurement system.
- The `SystemHealthMonitor` constraints.
- The `check_monetization_gate()` logic.
- The `PlayerProfile` data structure.

The system is infinitely scalable. Content is just data. The Iris is just a lens.
