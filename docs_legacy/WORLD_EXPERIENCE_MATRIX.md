# 2 SECOND WITNESS: WORLD EXPERIENCE MATRIX
*Validation of the World-as-Experiential-Layer Architecture*

This matrix defines the sensory, emotional, and interactive differentiation between Worlds within the same Universe. The goal is to prove that identical cognitive challenges (e.g., *Stroop Test*) feel fundamentally different based on the active World.

---

## UNIVERSE: HISTORY
*Foundation:* Warm palettes, low-frequency audio, "diagnostic" framing.

### World A: Ancient Egypt
**The Vibe:** Sun-baked, mystical, slow-burning tension.
- **Tunnel Profile:** Dense fog (`fog_density: 1.5`), slow undulating flow (`flow_type: wave`). Colors skew towards gold and deep shadow (`#E6C229`).
- **Iris Profile:** *Eye of Horus Engraving.* The lens counter-rotates slowly, like a heavy stone dial.
- **Audio Profile:** High ambient wind/sand noise. UI interactions (clicks, slingshots) are pitch-shifted down (`sfx_pitch: 0.7`) to sound heavy and resonant, like stone blocks sliding.
- **Cognitive Presentation (Interaction):** *Artifact Recovery.* Scenarios unfold slowly. Buttons (e.g., in a Stroop Test) drift upward from the bottom of the screen as if being excavated from the fog.

### World B: Roman Empire
**The Vibe:** Imperial, rigid, aggressive, structural.
- **Tunnel Profile:** Minimal fog (`fog_density: 0.8`), harsh straight lines (`flow_type: linear`). Colors skew towards blood red and iron (`#D90429`).
- **Iris Profile:** *Laurel Wreath Engraving.* The lens is sharp, mechanical, and expands aggressively.
- **Audio Profile:** Rhythmic, marching ambience. UI interactions are sharp, metallic, and clashing (`sfx_pitch: 1.1`), resembling swords or armor.
- **Cognitive Presentation (Interaction):** *Timeline Navigation.* Scenarios snap into place instantly. UI elements are locked to a rigid grid, reinforcing the feeling of imperial order and strict boundaries.

## UNIVERSE: SCIENCE LAB
*Foundation:* Cold palettes, high-frequency audio, "clinical" framing.

### World C: Physics
**The Vibe:** Energetic, unpredictable, mathematical chaos.
- **Tunnel Profile:** Medium fog (`fog_density: 1.0`), erratic paths (`flow_type: branching`). Colors skew towards bright cyan and absolute black (`#00F5FF`).
- **Iris Profile:** *Atomic Orbitals.* The lens features erratic, intersecting rings moving at different speeds.
- **Audio Profile:** High-pitched, electrical buzzing and zapping. UI interactions are extremely sharp and digitized (`sfx_pitch: 1.3`).
- **Cognitive Presentation (Interaction):** *Velocity Trajectories.* The UI itself is unstable. Buttons in the *Memory Cascade* drift across the screen while the user tries to memorize them, adding spatial tracking load to the memory load.

### World D: Astronomy
**The Vibe:** Vast, isolating, awe-inspiring, silent.
- **Tunnel Profile:** Almost no fog (`fog_density: 0.2`), slow spiral (`flow_type: vortex`). Colors skew towards deep purple and stellar white (`#9D4EDD`).
- **Iris Profile:** *Constellation Map.* The lens is barely visible, constructed of connected, glowing points of light.
- **Audio Profile:** Near silence. A very low, deep sub-bass drone. UI interactions echo heavily (`reverb_send` increased), simulating the vastness of space.
- **Cognitive Presentation (Interaction):** *Spatial Scale.* The UI is massive. Buttons are placed far apart on the screen, forcing the user's eyes to scan the entire canvas to find the correct answer, emphasizing distance and isolation.
