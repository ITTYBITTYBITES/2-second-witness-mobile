# Phase 0 Store Update Notes — Witness Foundation Shell

**Purpose:** prepare Google Play messaging recommendations only.  
**Status:** Do not publish from this document. Store claims must match the signed build after device validation.

---

## Positioning Direction

Phase 0 is a refinement release. Messaging should emphasize that Two Second Witness is now organized around the moment of discovery, not that new modes or future roadmap features are available.

Recommended positioning:

> Two Second Witness has been redesigned around the moment of discovery: open the app, begin an observation, and build your private Witness Record.

Avoid claims about unreleased systems such as Witness Threads, Story Mode, advanced evidence boards, new challenge types, social features, or progression economies.

---

## Suggested Release Notes

Short version:

> Two Second Witness has been refined around the Witness Moment. The app now opens to a clearer observation-focused home, with simpler navigation for Witness, Record, and Settings. Existing challenge content remains available through Explore Experiences.

Expanded version:

- Refocused Home around your next observation.
- Simplified primary navigation to Witness, Record, and Settings.
- Renamed Profile as Witness Record to better reflect personal observation history.
- Kept Challenge Library available as Explore Experiences.
- Added foundation work for future evidence reveal presentation.
- Preserved offline play, local progress, privacy posture, accessibility settings, and existing challenge content.

## Feature Graphic Direction

Recommended visual direction:

- dark editorial background;
- one warm scene/evidence focal area;
- restrained Witness eye/attention mark;
- minimal copy, e.g. “What did you miss?” or “Observe what others miss.”

Avoid:

- crime-board imagery;
- case-file/story promises;
- leaderboard/ranking visuals;
- reward/economy motifs;
- busy multi-mode menu collage.

## Store Listing Copy Direction

### Short description candidate

> A premium offline observation game about noticing what was always there.

### Full description adjustment direction

Lead with:

- short observation moments;
- fair recall;
- evidence returning in context;
- private Witness Record;
- offline/no-account experience.

Do not lead with:

- brain-training claims;
- IQ or diagnostic language;
- social competition;
- streak pressure;
- unreleased story or Threads language.

---

## Do Not Advertise Yet

Do not mention the following until implemented, validated, and present in the signed build:

- Witness Threads;
- Story Mode;
- full Evidence Reveal transformation;
- new Scene Quality pipeline assets;
- new challenge types;
- accounts/social/leaderboards;
- economies, currencies, shops, or rewards.
